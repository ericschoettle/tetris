TETRIS

Welcome to our Tetris game. We hope you have fun!

How to Play

1. Use the arrow keys to move and rotate the pieces along the board.
1a) Right arrow key will move your puzzle piece to the right
1b) Left arrow key will move your puzzle piece to the left
1c) Down arrow key will move your puzzle piece down faster than the timer
1d) Up arrow key will rotate your puzzle piece
2. Stack puzzle pieces.
